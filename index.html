About the School

A heritage of learning, character and service

Founded in 2018 in Ortum, St Josephs Onoch Mixed Boarding Senior School has grown into a trusted centre for academic excellence and the holistic development of young people in Kenya.

Our History

Established in 2018, St Josephs Onoch Mixed Boarding Senior School began with a single class of pioneering students determined to make a difference. Today, the school stands as a vibrant learning community shaped by the values of discipline, integrity and academic ambition — a place where every learner is challenged to grow into their best self.

The Badge & Motto

Our badge carries the torch of knowledge above an open book — the light of learning that never goes out. The laurels stand for honour and achievement, while the maroon shield and gold trim speak to courage and excellence. Beneath them flies our motto: Strive To Excel.

Principal's Message

"At Onoch we believe every student carries a flame of potential. Our role is to nurture that flame with knowledge, character, and care — preparing each learner for global citizenship."

Admissions

Join the Onoch family

We welcome applications from students ready to embrace our motto — Strive To Excel.

Admission Requirements

  • KCPE / Grade 6 results slip (original & copy)
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Two passport-size photos
  • Leaving certificate from previous school
  • Completed admission form

Application Process

  1. 1Download and complete the admission form
  2. 2Submit form with required documents
  3. 3Attend interview and orientation
  4. 4Receive admission letter and report dates
